20.01.2016 00:00
The largest prime number was just found, and it is a Mersenne prime, or a prime number of the form 2n - 1. As of January 2016, 274,207,281 - 1 is the largest known prime. Primes are interesting in their own right, but they also are indispensible in cryptography.
