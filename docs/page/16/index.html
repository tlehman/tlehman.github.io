There is a structure in abstract algebra called a monoid. There are several ways to define a monoid, but before we start, we should answer the obvious question: why should you care? The reason being aware of monoids is important is that they are everywhere, and knowing the properties of general monoids will lead to better understanding of their specific manifestations, such as the accumulator pattern or string concatenation.
